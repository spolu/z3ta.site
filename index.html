z3ta: towards safer systems design

z3ta's mission is to ease and extend the use of program and design verification to help engineers build safer systems.

We believe there is a strong latent need for program verification in tech companies, in particular infrastructural companies seeking very high levels of reliability. These companies are today structurally incapable of achieving more than 99.99% of reliability because the development tools popular in this decade (type-checking, unit and functional testing) do not provide the guarantees needed to achieve higher levels of reliability at scale (1000s of engineers collaborating on a single codebase).
